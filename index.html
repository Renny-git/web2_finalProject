Movies and series that
pass the Bechdel Test

Alison Bechde is an American cartoonist who, amongst other great things,
came up with this genius way of filtering movies to watch based on 3 simple rules:

    1. The movie should have at least two female characters who
    2. speak to each other about
    3. anything other than a man.

Below is a compilation of films and tv series that pass the “test”, enjoy!
